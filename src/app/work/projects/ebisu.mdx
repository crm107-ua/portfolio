## Overview

SaaS platform to manage a business's sales and payments: full transaction history, advanced analytics dashboard, ticketing support, and a public API for integrations. Developed as a University of Alicante project.

## Key Features

- **Transaction history**: Complete record of sales and payment events.
- **Analytics dashboard**: Business metrics and visual insights.
- **Secure payments**: Payment processing with audit trail.
- **Public REST API**: Endpoints for third-party integrations.

## Target Audience

Small and medium businesses and merchants that need to track sales, payments and transactions with analytics in one dashboard.

## Business Opportunity

SaaS subscription tiers, with potential transaction/processing fees and paid API access for third-party integrations.

- **Frontend:** Blade templates + jQuery
