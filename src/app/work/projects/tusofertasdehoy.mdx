## Overview

Web platform that aggregates and displays daily product deals and offers with affiliate links. Users can browse curated deals, compare prices, and follow outbound links to partner stores.

## Key Features

- **Deals listing**: Daily refreshed catalog of product offers.
- **Product cards with prices**: Structured cards with pricing and deal metadata.
- **Affiliate integration**: Outbound tracking links to partner retailers.

## Target Audience

Bargain hunters and deal seekers in Spain looking for daily curated product offers.

## Business Opportunity

Affiliate commissions (Amazon and others) plus display advertising. The n8n automation keeps content fresh at near-zero marginal cost, which makes the model scalable.

- **Backend:** Node.js 18+
