## Overview

Olvida is a platform that helps people reduce their digital footprint on the internet. It automates data-removal and opt-out processes against third parties through legally-compliant workflows, always executed under the user's express authorization. The goal is to give individuals back control over their personal data with minimal effort on their side.

## Key Features

- Automated data-removal / opt-out workflows
- Legally-compliant processes with explicit user consent
- Clean, accessible UI built on a modern component system
